The Negotiation

It was night. Broken glass scattered all over the floor, drops of blood here and there and wind blowing through the broken doors.

Policemen whispered while taking cover behind walls, tables and chairs. Connor followed the trail of blood that led to the balcony.
As Connor stepped out the silence was broken with a gunshot accompanied with a shout.
”Stay back ! Don't come any closer or I'll jump” the deviant shouted as the girl begged for her life,
he held her tight in his arms and stood on the edge of the balcony.”
Hi Daniel,my name is Connor” Connor replied.

“How do you know my name? ” Daniel shouted, pressing the girl tight onto his chest, squeezing onto the side of her head with the barrel of his gun.

“I know a lot of things about you, I've come to get you out of this” Connor calmly replied, as he slowly approached Daniel.

Unexpectedly a police chopper appeared in the sky disrupting the conversation, wind blew hard sending chairs flying across the balcony.

Connor continued to approach Daniel trying to reassure him “I am not going to hurt you” he said. “I just wanna talk and find a solution”.

“I dont wanna talk, it is too late for that.” Daniel said.”Are you armed !” he shouted pointing the gun towards Connor.

Connor’s choice: Lies

Connor: “No, I don't have a gun.”
Daniel:”You’re lying! ”(Daniel shouts back).
Connor: “I’m telling you the truth.”(Connor says persuasively).
Connor: “I came here unarmed. They were going to replace you and you became upset, that is what happened right?”

Daniel:”But i was nothing to them”.(angrily said squeezing the gun to Emma's head)
Daniel:”Ughhhhhh… I can’t stand that noise anymore!” (he says,complaining about the chopper).
Daniel:”Tell that helicopter to get out of here.”(he orders aggressively).
Connor signals the helicopter to leave.
